About Me

Entreprenur

I'm Amit Purohit, co-founder of Swag ERT and Swag Bikes, and a Colorado State University graduate. Committed to sustainability, I'm revolutionizing transportation. As part of the "Breathing Bangalore" program, I supply oxygen cylinders to homes, aiding those in need. With a focus on eco-friendly solutions, my aim is to reduce emissions and improve urban mobility. Technology and entrepreneurship are powerful tools for change, and I strive to make a lasting impact on individuals and communities. Join me in shaping the future of transportation, creating a greener world, and improving the well-being of all. Together, let's make a difference.
